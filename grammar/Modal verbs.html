Modalverben / Modalverbs
S.No. Deutsch English
1 können can / to be able to
2 wollen to want (to)
3 müßen must / to have to
4 dürfen may / to be permitted to / to be allowed to
5 sollen should
6 möchten would like to

Rule: When you use modal verb in a sentence, only modal verb should be conjugated.
