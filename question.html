German (Deutsch) is the official language in Germany, Austria and Switzerland and is widely spoken in Liechtenstein, and the Italian province of South Tyrol. It is also a co-official language of Luxembourg and Belgium. Every year the percentage of those who want to learn German almost goes up. People learn German mainly for job, business and higher education in Germany (+ DACH). Germany offers a lot of great things, with those one can get off to a good start. Whether it is a scientific career or IT - Engineer career or medical career or any professional career, you will find it in Germany. It is highly recommended to learn German (need not be very fluent but at least basic level of A2), if you want to be more successful in your business in Germany.
